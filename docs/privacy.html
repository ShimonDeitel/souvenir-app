Privacy Policy

Souvenir Book is an offline-first app. All data you enter is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data, usage analytics, or identifiers. There are no third-party trackers or advertising SDKs in this app.

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit; we never see your payment details.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is removed with it.
